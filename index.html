MARIAM PARVEZ SHEIKH

Amherst, MA · Palo Alto, CA

Hello! My name is Mariam and I am a Ph.D. candidate at the College of Education at the University of Massachusetts Amherst.

For more about me, please see here.

mpsheikh@umass.edu

My current research project is a study of how young students in higher education of Pakistan produce alternative knowledge as they articulate various struggles including climate justice, feminism and anti-capitalism. In doing so, it examines how they expose gender, ethnic and class inequities through their resistance and struggle for a socially just higher education system.

I have been a unit planner and created several panels with the South Asia Special Interest Group (SASIG) at the Comparative & International Education Society (CIES).
